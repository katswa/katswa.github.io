Sketch

Using a pencil, I plan and plot out the major elements that will make up the artwork. Different sections are given less or more detail, depending on whether they will be covered or followed as a guide.

Major Colors

The largest rudamentary shapes of color are filled, giving me an idea of the piece as I work. My original plan may be altered slightly in this phase as I realize better solutions to conveying my idea.

Finish

The final smallest details are added and adjusted, with critique from family and peers to assist. Details can consist of shading, contour, and any colored pencil or pen work.

Closeup

A closer look at the focal point, where most of the detail has been oriented in order to attract focus. The materials used for this project were as such:

  • Acryllics for the backdrop, lights, and keys
  • Oil pastels for the waves of variation
  • Colored pencil for the cage object itself
  • Pasted sheet music for the twisting keyboard
  • Finally white pen for the stars and highlights
